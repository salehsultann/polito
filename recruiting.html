Recruting 2024

Are you passionate about boating and/or sustainable propulsion? This is the perfect opportunity for you! We are the PoliTo H2Fly team, and we are designing and building a hydrogen-powered boat to demonstrate the potential of clean energy in marine applications. Our ambitious project is born with the idea of participating in the Monaco Solar & Energy Boat Challenge in July 2024, organized by the Yacht Club de Monaco. We are excited to announce that our model has already been accepted by the technical committee!

We are a new team, born just over six months ago, looking for people but, most importantly, for new ideas. If you want to explore this sector, perhaps with some friends, we are ready to welcome you.

We are looking for:

  • Pilot: requires a boating license;
  • Graphic and video manager and staff;
  • Social media management (Instagram and LinkedIn), event coordination, sponsor research;
  • Driveline staff: fuel cell, batteries, and outboard motor;
  • Mechanical structure staff;
  • Electronics for control and data acquisition staff;

Think you don't fit into any category? Contact us anyway, and we'll figure out together how you can help! Your experience and creativity will be crucial to our mission. If we've grabbed your attention and you want to be part of this initiative, we invite you to consider joining us. If you want to learn more about the PoliTo H2Fly project, follow us on our social channels and fill out the form – we'll get in touch with you as soon as possible!

You have until March 24th!
